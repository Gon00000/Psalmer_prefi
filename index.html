First, locate the small rectangular object your children have been hiding from you for the past 3 weeks. This mystical device is called a "remote." If you can't find it, check under the couch cushions, inside the refrigerator, or your dog's secret treasure collection.

Why does my TV have more buttons than my remote?

Modern TVs are actually designed by sadistic engineers who get a bonus every time someone throws a remote across the room in frustration. The extra buttons are there to make you feel inadequate about your technological capabilities.

What's the best TV size to buy?

The formula is simple: Take the square footage of your living room, multiply by your credit card limit, divide by your spouse's patience, and subtract any remaining wall space where family photos once hung. The resulting number is still not big enough.
